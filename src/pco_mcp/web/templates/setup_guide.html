Add Planning Center to ChatGPT

Heads up: Because this is a custom connector, you'll need to turn on ChatGPT's Developer mode first. Available on ChatGPT Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise, and Edu plans.

  1. Open ChatGPT Settings

    Click your profile icon in ChatGPT, then choose Settings.

  2. Go to Apps

    Click Apps in the left sidebar.

  3. Open Advanced settings

    Click Advanced settings (near the bottom).

  4. Turn on Developer mode

    Toggle Developer mode on. This lets you add custom connectors like Planning Center. (ChatGPT will warn you about "elevated risk" — that's expected for any custom connector.)

  5. Click "Create app"

    Go back to the Apps screen and click Create app.

  6. Fill in the details

    • Name: Planning Center
    • MCP Server URL: paste the connection link from your dashboard
    • Authentication: select OAuth

  7. Authorize Planning Center

    ChatGPT will send you to Planning Center's login page. Sign in and click Authorize. You'll land back in ChatGPT with Planning Center connected.

  8. Start using it

    Open a new chat and turn on the Planning Center app from the chat's tool menu. Then ask ChatGPT anything about your church data.

Example questions you can ask

  • "Who are the volunteers at our church?"
  • "What songs are scheduled for this Sunday?"
  • "Show me upcoming service plans"
  • "Find the contact info for John Smith"
