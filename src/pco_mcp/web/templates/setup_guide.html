Add Planning Center to ChatGPT

  1. Open ChatGPT Settings

    In ChatGPT, click your profile icon, then "Settings".

  2. Go to Apps

    Click "Apps" in the left sidebar.

  3. Create a new App

    Click "Create" and give it a name like "Planning Center".

  4. Paste your MCP URL

    Paste the URL from your dashboard into the URL field.

  5. Done!

    Start a new chat and ask ChatGPT about your Planning Center data.

Example questions you can ask

  • "Who are the volunteers at our church?"
  • "What songs are scheduled for this Sunday?"
  • "Show me upcoming service plans"
  • "Find the contact info for John Smith"
